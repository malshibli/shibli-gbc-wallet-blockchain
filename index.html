How to Get Started with SHIBLI GBC

  1. Install MetaMask Extension:
    Visit https://metamask.io/download and install the MetaMask wallet on your browser or mobile device.
  2. Create Your Wallet:
    - Open MetaMask and click “Create a Wallet”.
    - Set your secure password and back up your recovery phrase securely.
  3. Select Sepolia Test Network:
    - Click the top dropdown in MetaMask.
    - Enable Show Test Networks from MetaMask Settings > Advanced.
    - Select “Sepolia Testnet”.
  4. Connect Your Wallet to SHIBLI GBC:
    - Click the “Connect MetaMask” button on this page.
    - Approve the connection from MetaMask popup.
  5. Import SHIBLI GBC Token:
    - In MetaMask, click “Import Token”
    - Use this Contract Address: 0x8357E2760CBd9867399b448DD11ab3458CaEcf93
    - Token Symbol: GBC
    - Token Decimals: 18
    - Click “Add Custom Token” and “Import”.
  6. Request GBC Tokens:
    - Fill in the Request Form (coming soon).
    - Or contact our community support to request your free GBC allocation as part of our birthright initiative.
  7. Send/Receive GBC:
    - Enter a recipient wallet address and GBC amount.
    - Click “Send GBC”.
    - Transaction will process through MetaMask and be visible in your activity log.

Need help? Contact our support team or explore the FAQ section (coming soon).
